Communication

Communication comes first because without it, we can't get anything done.

GitHub

If you do not already have a GitHub account, create one. Ideally choose a username that makes it easy for others to identify you, and remember that your GitHub account is going to be a part of your professional identity, so choose wisely.

Email Ryaan at rahmed@mit.edu with your username, so he can add you to our organization.

Even if you already have a GitHub account, you may wish to sign up for the Student Developer Pack.

Slack

Join Slack, and join the mitdhlab workspace.

Once you've joined the workspace, send us a 'hello world!' message on the #general channel, so everyone knows how to reach you there.

Email

As stated in in the UROP Contract, we expect you to check your email once per business day and expect that you will respond to messages in a timely manner.

See Contact for potentially useful email addresses.
